About Me

I am a second-year architecture student who enjoys exploring how spaces can shape community and experience. I am fascinated by travel, and I bring ideas from different places into my work.

In design, I focus on thoughtful materials, balanced forms, and how people move through a building. I believe architecture should be welcoming and promote connection between people.

Beyond school, I love learning new tools like Rhino and Revit, and I enjoy working with others to build strong relationships. My goal is to create work that is meaningful, calm, and carefully considered.

This site is my portfolio for sharing projects, ideas, and progress as I grow in architecture and design.
